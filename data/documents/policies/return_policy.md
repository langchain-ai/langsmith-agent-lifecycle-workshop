# Return Policy

At TechHub, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we accept returns within our specified return windows.

## Return Windows

**Unopened Electronics**
- 30 days from delivery date
- All original packaging and seals must be intact
- Full refund to original payment method

**Opened Electronics**
- 14 days from delivery date
- Product must be in good working condition
- All accessories, cables, and documentation must be included
- Original packaging helpful but not required

**All Other Items**
- 30 days from delivery date
- Must be in original condition

## Condition Requirements

To qualify for a return, items must meet the following conditions:

- Product is in good working condition with no physical damage
- All original accessories are included (power adapters, cables, manuals, etc.)
- No signs of misuse or abuse
- Serial numbers and identification labels are intact
- For opened items, minimal signs of use are acceptable

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

- Clearance or final sale items (marked as such at checkout)
- Digital gift cards
- Downloadable software or digital products
- Items marked "non-returnable" on the product page

## Restocking Fees

**No Restocking Fee:**
- Defective items (manufacturer defects or damage during shipping)
- Unopened items in original packaging
- Items under $500

**15% Restocking Fee:**
- Opened electronics over $500 being returned due to buyer's remorse
- Fee is calculated on the purchase price and deducted from your refund
- Does not apply to defective items or our errors

## Return Process

1. **Contact Customer Support**  
   Reach out via phone (1-800-555-TECH), email (support@techhub.com), or live chat within the return window

2. **Receive RMA Number**  
   We'll provide a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and return instructions

3. **Prepare Your Package**  
   Pack the item securely with all accessories included. Write the RMA number clearly on the outside of the package

4. **Ship the Item**  
   - For defective items: Use the prepaid return label we provide
   - For other returns: Customer is responsible for return shipping costs
   - We recommend using a trackable shipping method with insurance

5. **Refund Processing**  
   Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

## Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase:
- Credit/debit cards: 5-7 business days to appear on your statement
- PayPal: 3-5 business days
- Original shipping charges are refunded for defective items only

## Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. To exchange an item:
1. Return the original item following the process above
2. Place a new order for the replacement item

This ensures you receive your replacement as quickly as possible rather than waiting for the return to be processed first.

## Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:
- Contact us immediately (within 48 hours of delivery for shipping damage)
- Provide photos of the damage if possible
- We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund at no cost to you
- Return shipping is free for defective items

- Email: support@techhub.com (response within 24 hours)
- Live Chat: Available on our website during business hours
