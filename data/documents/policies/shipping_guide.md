# Shipping Guide

Fast, reliable shipping is our priority. Here's everything you need to know about getting your order delivered.

## Shipping Options

**Standard Shipping (5-7 Business Days)**
- FREE on orders $50 or more
- $7.99 flat rate on orders under $50
- Available to all 50 US states
- Tracking included

**Express Shipping (2-3 Business Days)**
- $14.99 flat rate on all orders
- Expedited processing and delivery
- Signature may be required for high-value items
- Tracking included

Note: Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding federal holidays.

## Order Processing Time

Before shipping, orders go through our processing and quality control:

- **Orders placed before 2 PM ET (Monday-Friday):** Ship same business day
- **Orders placed after 2 PM ET:** Ship next business day
- **Weekend orders:** Processed on Monday
- **Processing time:** 1-2 business days for most items

During busy periods (holidays, sales events), processing may take an additional 1-2 business days.

## Tracking Your Order

**Email Notifications:**
- Order confirmation sent immediately after purchase
- Shipping confirmation with tracking number sent when order ships
- Check your spam folder if you don't see these emails

**Track Your Package:**
- Use the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
- Visit the carrier website (UPS, FedEx, USPS) with your tracking number
- Log into your TechHub account and check "My Orders"
- Tracking updates every 4-6 hours from the carrier

## Shipping Carriers

We partner with reliable carriers to ensure safe delivery:

- **UPS:** Most standard and express shipments
- **FedEx:** Express shipments and certain regions
- **USPS:** Economy options and APO/FPO addresses

We select the best carrier for your location and order type. You'll see the carrier information when your order ships.

## Delivery

**What to Expect:**
- Packages require a signature for orders over $500 (you can sign electronically or in-person)
- Delivery is made to the shipping address provided at checkout
- Drivers may leave packages at the door if signature is not required and the location appears secure

**Not Home for Delivery?**
- Carriers typically make up to 3 delivery attempts
- You can sign up for carrier notifications to manage delivery (hold at location, reschedule, etc.)
- Check your tracking information for specific carrier options

## Shipping Restrictions

**Currently Available:**
- All 50 US states (including Alaska and Hawaii)
- APO/FPO addresses (via USPS)

**Not Currently Available:**
- International shipping
- US territories (Puerto Rico, Guam, US Virgin Islands)
- PO Boxes (for items requiring signature)

We're working to expand our shipping options. Check back for updates.

## Delivery Issues

**Package Not Received:**
- Check with household members or building management
- Look around delivery location (porch, side door, mailroom)
- Check tracking for delivery confirmation and location notes
- Contact the carrier using your tracking number
- If still not located after 48 hours, contact us for assistance

**Package Damaged:**
- Inspect packages upon delivery when possible
- For visible damage, refuse the delivery or note damage with the driver
- For concealed damage, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos
- We'll arrange for a replacement or refund

**Wrong Address:**
- Contact us immediately if you entered the wrong shipping address
- We may be able to correct it before shipment
- Once shipped, address changes must go through the carrier
- Address changes may incur additional fees from the carrier

**Package Marked as Delivered But Not Received:**
- Check around your delivery location carefully
- Wait 24 hours (sometimes marked delivered early)
- Contact the carrier for GPS delivery location
- Contact us if not resolved within 48 hours

## Tips for Smooth Delivery

1. **Double-check your address** at checkout including apartment/unit numbers
2. **Provide delivery instructions** if your location is hard to find
3. **Enable carrier notifications** to track delivery in real-time
4. **Be available** on delivery day for high-value items requiring signature
5. **Inspect packages** promptly after delivery
